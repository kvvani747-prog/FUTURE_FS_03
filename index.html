Our Story

Crafted with Love,
Worn with Confidence

Auric Drape was born in 2016 from a simple belief — every woman deserves to feel extraordinary. Founded by Varsha Gowda, a textile enthusiast with a deep love for Indian craftsmanship, our boutique began as a small studio in Indiranagar and has grown into a beloved destination for women across Bengaluru.

We source our fabrics directly from weavers in Pochampally, Kanchipuram, and Lucknow — ensuring authenticity, quality, and a story behind every thread. Whether it's a daily wear kurta or a grand wedding ensemble, each piece at Auric Drape is chosen with intention.

Our philosophy is simple: fashion should be personal, sustainable, and timeless. We believe in slow fashion — not fast trends.
