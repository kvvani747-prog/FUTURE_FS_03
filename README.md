## 🏪 About the Project
Auric Drape is a professional website built for a local women's boutique based in Indiranagar, Bengaluru. This project was built as part of the Future Interns Full Stack Web Development internship task to demonstrate how a real local business can establish its online presence.

## 💡 Problem It Solves
- Gives the boutique a professional online identity
- Attracts new customers through collections and styling sessions
- Builds trust through testimonials and contact details
- Makes it easy for customers to reach the business via WhatsApp, email, and contact form

## 🛠️ Built With
- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript (Vanilla)
- Google Fonts (Playfair Display + Raleway)
- GitHub Pages (Deployment)

## 📄 Pages & Sections
- Hero Section with CTA buttons
- About Us & Founder Story
- Collections (Ethnic, Western, Fusion)
- Featured Products
- Why Choose Us
- Customer Testimonials
- Photo Gallery
- Contact Form + Google Maps
- Footer with social links
